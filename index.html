What we do

Everything your car needs, under one roof

From a quick WoF to a major repair, we keep Christchurch cars running safely — and we explain the work in plain language, not workshop jargon.

01

WoF Inspections

Warrant of Fitness checks while you wait, with a clear rundown of any fails and what it takes to put them right.

02

Servicing & Maintenance

Logbook and routine servicing to keep your car reliable and your warranty intact. Oil, filters, fluids — the lot.

03

Brakes & Suspension

Squealing, pulling or a spongy pedal? We diagnose and replace pads, discs, shocks and struts so you stop when you should.

04

Tyres & Wheel Alignment

New tyres, balancing and four-wheel alignment to stop uneven wear and keep your car tracking straight.

Diagnostics

Engine light on? We plug in and read the fault properly, so you fix the actual problem — not a guess.

06

Pre-Purchase Checks

Thinking of buying? We give a used car the once-over and tell you honestly whether it's worth the money.

About us

A workshop that treats your car like it's our own

Spreydon Motors is a local, independent garage looking after everyday Christchurch drivers. We're not a faceless chain — when you ring up, you talk to the people actually working on your car.

Our promise is simple: we'll tell you what needs doing now, what can safely wait, and exactly what it'll cost before we go ahead. No upsell, no surprises when you come to collect.

  • Locally owned and operated in Spreydon
  • Clear, up-front quotes on every job
  • All makes & models — petrol, diesel and hybrid
