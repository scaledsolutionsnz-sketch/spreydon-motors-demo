About us

A workshop that treats your car like it's our own

Spreydon Motors is a local, independent garage looking after everyday Christchurch drivers. We're not a faceless chain — when you ring up, you talk to the people actually working on your car.

Our promise is simple: we'll tell you what needs doing now, what can safely wait, and exactly what it'll cost before we go ahead. No upsell, no surprises when you come to collect.

  • Locally owned and operated in Spreydon
