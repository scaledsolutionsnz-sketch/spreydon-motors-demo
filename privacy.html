Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026 · Spreydon Motors, Christchurch

This is placeholder wording for the demo and is not legal advice. Final policy should be reviewed before going live.

1. What we collect

When you contact us through this website we collect the details you give us — your name, phone number, email, vehicle details and your message. That's it. We don't track you around the web.

2. How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, book your vehicle in, and provide the service you've asked for. We don't sell it, and we don't share it with third parties for marketing.

3. Form handling

Our contact and feedback forms are delivered by FormSubmit, which forwards your message to our email inbox. Their handling of your data is subject to their own privacy practices.

4. Storage & security

We keep enquiry information for as long as needed to help you and to meet our record-keeping obligations, then dispose of it securely. We take reasonable steps to protect the information we hold.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Just get in touch and we'll help.
